Meet The Children

Jabari, Aged: 4

Jabari has to walk for miles to collect a bag of food and drag it along the ground through dirt, gravel, stone, trash and garbage left on the side of the road and on the path. Once Javari arrives at his "home" its nothing but a pile of rubbish that he lays on as a bed and eats the food raw from the bag.

Lebna, Aged: 3

Lebna has no parents and has been living in the streets without food for months. He has to live amongst the garbage on the ground, he has no way of clean water to clean himself or to drink. He might not even survive another few days please help.

Panya, Aged: 1

Panya has been living in the streets with other like minded impoverished children of which Panya needed to beg for food and water from people and shes only one year old which is shocking. Please help Panya with food and water with your donation.
