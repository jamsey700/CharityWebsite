EMERGENCY COUNTRIES

From catastrophic natural disasters, to conflict, to spikes in malnutrition rates, we deliver immediate lifesaving services to families in distress all around the world

YEMEN CAN'T WAIT

As violence in Yemen nears its fourth year, the country is facing the world’s worst humanitarian crisis in decades. More than 14 million people are facing starvation and an estimated 85,000 children may have died from extreme hunger since the start of the conflict.

EDUCATION OR FOOD? IN AFGHANISTAN

There is little hope for many who want to leave the refugee camps in the capital city of Afghanistan, Kabul. Their children's survival, rather than their education, is the priority of many mothers in Afghanistan. The children sell plastic bags and contribute with a meager income.
