Get Involved to End World Hunger! Regardless of your location on the map, age, schedule or resources, we believe you can make a positive difference in the fight against hunger. Even seemingly small contributions can add up to a BIG impact. We’d like to invite you to take action, donate and join our growing movement to end hunger worldwide by 2030. Here are a few ways you can get involved.

DONATE

Make an immediate impact by giving to Good Causes Inc. Financial contributions help us reach even more people around the world. Your donation will go toward the area of greatest need, which may include community empowerment, direct feeding programs or other life-changing programs.

ENGAGE YOUR WORKPLACE

Good Causes Inc provides many opportunities to engage our corporate partners and their employees in our mission to end hunger. Our Corporate Team works with companies to help them reach their CSR and employee engagement goals as we serve those in need around the world. We’d love to speak with you about partnership opportunities.

VOLUNTEER

Volunteers are one of the essential resources Good Causes Inc needs to help realize the vision of a world without hunger. Each year, thousands of volunteers join Good Causes Inc not only to package meals, but to support warehouse operations and assist with administrative duties. Find a Good Causes Inc location near you to find out how get involved and end world hunger.

JOURNEY TO SERVE

Through Journey to Serve, Good Causes Inc's partnership with Global Volunteers, you can join like-minded people from all walks of life on a journey to explore how you can deepen your global impact on a service trip abroad. Help end hunger, create food security, improve nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture on a global scale — all in just one to two weeks.
